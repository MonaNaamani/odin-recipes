Potato pancakes

Description

Because who doesn't love potatoes? Or pancakes!

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • onion
  • egg
  • salt and pepper
  • flour
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Grate the potatoes and the onion.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the potatoes and onion with an egg, some flour and salt and pepper to taste.
  3. The mix should not be too watery.
  4. On a frying pan, heat up some oil.
  5. Using a spoon, form small pancakes on the pan.
  6. Once golden brown, serve the pancakes with some cream and sugar.
